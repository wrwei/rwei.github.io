I am blessed to be working closely with some very supportive friends, whom are an important part of my life.

Prof Zhe Jiang

Zhe is currently an academic faculty at the School of Integrated Circuits, Southeast University, Nanjing, China. He is broadly interested in the architecture, micro-architecture, and design automation for ermerging computing systems, with a particular focus on improving their safety, security, reliability, and timing-predictability. We are working together on assuring the safety of micro processors using state-of-the-art technologies such as MBSE and LLM.
